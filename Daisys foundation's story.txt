Help creating a mission and vision statement for Daisy's non profit Inc.  Who is Daisy? Daisy was my grandmother who grew up in Jamaica and later migrated the US. She had nine children and worked as a pediatric nurse before moving to the US.  While in the US she worked as a care-giver for families in the Miami area.  As a little girl my cousins and I visited my grand-mother Daisy frequently, usually after school and on the weekends when our parents were busy.  Grand-Mother always had time for her grand-children.  She knew all of her grandchildren favorite meals and desserts were and always have them ready when we arrived at her house.  She was extremely patient and loving with us, she created a safe space and was always ready to listen or give advise.  Because of my Grandmothers love and other amazing qualities, I wanted to be just like her.  At an early age I started caring for my younger cousins and dreamed of owning a school that provides the same love and attention we got from our grandmother.  That dream was realized in 2018 when I open the doors to my first childcare center.  Being in the early education business I witness the firsthand struggles of single mothers, teen-age mother, young parents who have to settle for sub-quality care due to affordability.  The main purpose of Daisy's Non-Profit is to help deserving individuals who are struggling to afford a quality pre-kinder garden program for their little ones.  Daisy's Non-Profit will help by subsidizing tuition, provide funding for educational enrichment and job training.  We believe that with a solid early education foundation and child will be set to better navigate future academic, social and emotional challenges.
