# Daisy Foundation — AI Photo List & NanoBanana Prompts

## General Style Notes (apply to ALL prompts)
- **Subjects**: African American people, warm and authentic expressions
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm, golden-hour or soft indoor lighting
- **Style**: Photorealistic, editorial quality, warm color tones
- **Mood**: Hopeful, loving, community-oriented, genuine
- **Daisy Theme**: White and yellow daisy flowers should appear in nearly every photo — in gardens, fields, bouquets, table arrangements, hair accessories, printed fabrics, or growing naturally in outdoor settings. Daisies are the visual identity of the foundation.
- **Setting Preference**: Outdoor community settings whenever possible — parks, gardens, porches, community centers with open doors
- **Avoid**: Stock photo feel, stiff poses, overly staged looks
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for backgrounds/heroes, 3:2 for cards, 1:1 for portraits
